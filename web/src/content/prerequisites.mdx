This workshop is deliberately light on prerequisites.

## You bring
- A laptop with a modern browser.
- An SSH client (built into macOS/Linux; Windows: OpenSSH or WSL) — optional, the website's shell covers most of it.
- Basic terminal comfort. No Kubernetes experience required.

## We provide — your Brev credits 🎟️
**NVIDIA Brev** hosts your instance. At the event we hand out **free Brev credits** — enough for the whole session. You redeem a coupon; no credit card, no personal cloud account.

<Callout type="tip" title="Credits cover everything">
The instance is a small CPU VM (no GPU), so it's inexpensive. Just <strong>stop your instance</strong> when you're done so you don't burn credits idling.
</Callout>

## You do NOT need
- ❌ A GPU — inference is a remote endpoint.
- ❌ A local Kubernetes/OpenShift install — it runs on the VM.
- ❌ A Red Hat subscription — we use the open-source OKD build of MicroShift.
- ❌ Deep YAML knowledge — the deploy is one command.
