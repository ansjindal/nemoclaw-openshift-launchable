Welcome to the **WeAreDevelopers 2026** hands-on workshop from **Red Hat** and **NVIDIA**. Over the next hour you'll stand up your *own* AI agent — running safely inside a real OpenShift cluster — then build something on top of it.

Everything runs on a single cloud VM with **no GPU and no Kubernetes experience required**. We hand you the Brev credits; you bring a laptop and a browser.

## How this works

This guide is a short book of **parts → chapters**. The hands-on chapters (🧪) include a **live shell right in the page** — when you click *Run in shell*, the command runs on your real instance.

- **Part I–II** — the platform we pre-built for you (cluster → OpenShell gateway), and how to read it
- **Part III** — understand and drive OpenShell (control plane + policies) before you build
- **Part IV** — hands-on: create your own OpenClaw agent on the gateway, step by step
- **Part V** — make it yours and operate it: identity, into the sandbox
- **Part VI** — the hackathon: build a useful **multi-agent** app

<Callout type="tip" title="Want to get hands-on fast?">
Skim Part I–II (the platform is already running) and jump to **Part IV · Build Your Agent**. The architecture chapters are there when you want the <em>why</em>.
</Callout>
